How InventoryMan works

    Its simple. We use a linear regression testing model (Artificial Intelligence) which does various forms of test and provides the prediction of how many products to purchase on a monthly basis.

    Step 1. Connect through a third-party service API call or POS data source to InventoryMan. If you are an RBC Employee, all you have to do is enter your inventory details through POS Data (CSV format).

    Step 2. You can even enter some sales manually through the button above.

    Step 3. See the predictions and get an accurate recommendation on how much of a product to purchase!


    RBC is able to view the data of your inventory overtime and can help craft a better RBC product. For example: an increase in RBC's Small Business Banking Loan, or improvements in your Businesss insurance rate!
